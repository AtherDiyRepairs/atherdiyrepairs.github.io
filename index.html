Ather Charging Flap Repair

Items Required: Any screwdriver and WD-40

Step 1: Start by opening the floor board as the plastic panel covering the charging flap is under the floor board. There are 8 screws to unscrew, and then gently remove the floor board.

Step 2: Then we will have to remove the front inside panel (above the floor panel). There are 12 screws to unscrew, and also remove the keyhole cover.

Now start by removing the botom as shwon in image, and then pulling the panel from the bottom to your side, and there will be a small plastic piece at the top which you have to look for just pull it up gentaly and wiggle the panel a bit and it will come out. And also, don't pull the panel so hard or it will get cracked. Also, there will be a wired connection behind that panel for the buzzer. Just remove the connector and take out the panel.

Step 3: Now you have the panel opened, just unscrew 4 bolts from behind as shown in the image below to remove the charging flap, and then just remove those 2 plastic pieces.

Step 4: Now in that plastic piece there will be a spring mechanism as shwon in image, just remove it by pulling it. Now just spray a good amount of WD-40 on it, and clean it after a minute. Now just put the spring mechanism back to its place as it was before[ Make sure to reatch the spring proper with the retainer,check the image below] and re-attach the plastic pieces and flap (you can easily attach the flap by keeping the flap in the open position)

Step 5: Now put everything back in reverse gently! And check yout chraging flap it will be working like a new again.
